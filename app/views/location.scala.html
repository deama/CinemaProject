Getting here

We are located at:

    Anchorage 1

    Anchorage Quay Salford

    The Quays

    Manchester

    M50 3YJ

There are several ways of getting here, all outlined below:

By car

We are easily located by leaving the M60 at J12, then following signs for The Quays.

We are at Anchorage Quay, which you can follow road signage to.

Or, you can come off the M60 at J7 and follow the A56 to The Quays and follow road signs to Anchorage Quay.

There is a car park adjacent to the building where you can easily find parking spaces.

By public transport

To get here via public transport, we are located at the Anchorage Quay Metrolink stop, or alternatively,

You can get one of the many bus services to nearby MediaCity UK, which run frequently between 6 am and 11 pm.

Spaceship access is unfortunately still under construction.
